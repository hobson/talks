Large dimensions are scary

4. TFIDF Similarity

Vector distances

  • L_1, L_2, [L_0, L_inf, L_sup]
  • Fractional Distance

4. TFIDF Similarity

Cosine Similarity (similarity = 1 / distance)

  • Equivalent:
    • Pierson Correlation
    • | v_1 dot v_2 | (projection)
    • angle between v1 and v2
  • Bounded: [-1, +1]
